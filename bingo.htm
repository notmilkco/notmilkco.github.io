HOWTO: Enhanced Bingo

All you need for your own spin of enhanced bingo is creativity and a willingness to stick to the rules you create consistently. if you don't know how to play bingo for some reason, we've provided a sample card to reference for the following instructions. also, here's a handy tutorial on how to play regular bingo*, and here is an example of human bingo* in practice.

Enhanced bingo goes a little something like this:

  1. Write in the name of the Target (it could be a single individual, a company, or any other entity/mix of entities really) in the "_____ Enhanced Bingo" field.

  2. make a list of words, things, phrases, or whatever else your target does/dosen't do, and make the list large enough to fill out all of the squares (and optionally a free space in the center) of a 5x5 or larger odd-numbered grid.

  3. decide on perks! we are constantly updating an example list of perks for you to choose from, but you can obviously come up with your own if you want! make sure everyone's on the same page about what the perk you come up with entails if you choose that route.

  4. Write the names of the perks you decided to use on the "Perks: _______" line. After that, you're good to mix cards and go!

NOTMILK! Enhanced Bingo:

This is essentially human bingo with perks.

we got the idea when one unnamed member
of the team wanted to be petty during a late
night raid, and made a "human bingo" card,
listing all of the things unnamed member A
was tired of hearing unnamed member B say.

a distraught member C decided to warn all
members involved that this could get toxic
if it kept going on for too long, and all
members invloved in the creation of the petty
3x3 bingo agreed.

We decided to not only
expand the contents/targets of the card to
include all members, but also made it dynamic
and scalable so that others could use it, and
here we are.
